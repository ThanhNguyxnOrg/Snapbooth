# SnapStrip Studio 📸

## 🎉 [**TRY IT LIVE**](https://thanhnguyxn.github.io/Photo-Booth/)

A free online photo booth with 12 creative templates. Create stunning photo strips in seconds!

---

## ✨ Features

- 🎭 **12 Creative Templates** - Newspaper, Magazine, Comic, Polaroid, Passport, Movie, Vintage, Neon, Scrapbook, Minimalist, Instagram, Classic
- 📷 **Camera or Upload** - Take photos with webcam or upload existing ones
- 🎨 **6 Photo Filters** - None, B&W, Sepia, Vintage, Cool, Warm
- 📥 **Download & Share** - Export as JPG or share to social media
- 📱 **Fully Responsive** - Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile
- 🔒 **Privacy First** - All processing in browser, no uploads
- 🆓 **100% Free** - No watermarks, no limits

## 🚀 Quick Start

Just visit: **[https://thanhnguyxn.github.io/Photo-Booth/](https://thanhnguyxn.github.io/Photo-Booth/)**

1. Click **START**
2. Choose **Layout** (2-6 poses)
3. Select **Template** (12 options)
4. **Capture** or **Upload** photos
5. **Download** your photo strip!
