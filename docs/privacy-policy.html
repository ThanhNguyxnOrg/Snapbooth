Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 11, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to SnapStrip Studio. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a safe and enjoyable experience when using our online photo booth service. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

Photos and Camera Access: When you use our photo booth service, you grant us temporary access to your device's camera. However, all photos are processed entirely in your browser and are never uploaded to our servers or stored anywhere unless you explicitly choose to download them.

Usage Data: We may collect non-personal information about how you interact with our website, such as pages visited, features used, and general usage statistics through analytics services.

Cookies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience, remember your preferences, and analyze site traffic.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our photo booth service
  • Improve and optimize our website functionality
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Communicate with you about updates, features, or support
  • Detect and prevent technical issues or security threats

Data Storage and Security

Your Photos Are Private: We want to emphasize that your photos are never uploaded to our servers. All photo processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your images remain on your device and are only saved if you choose to download them.

We implement appropriate security measures to protect any data we do collect (such as usage statistics) from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics, advertising, or other purposes. These services may collect information about your use of our website. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these third-party services:

  • Google Analytics - for website traffic analysis
  • Google AdSense - for displaying advertisements
  • PayPal - for processing donations

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Object to our processing of your information
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Camera Permissions

To use the photo booth feature, you must grant camera access to your browser. You can revoke this permission at any time through your browser settings. Denying camera access will prevent you from using the photo booth, but you can still browse other parts of our website.

Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

GDPR Compliance
